Welcome to the Job!

Welcome to Melty's! We are working on turning it into a arcade that rivals even Freddy's, but we are a little low on funds, so its pretty empty. Your job is to make sure that the animatronics don't make a mess of the office. Last weekend we had to buy a new desk because the previous guard just left early or something. No sign of him since, so you're here! Like I said, we are a little low on funds, so power is limited. Luckily, we have a backup generator. Just open the breaker box in front of you and you'll see a crank to power it on. It takes a bit, so just watch the power meter next to it to see if its done charging. I think I mentioned it already, but the animatronics do wander a bit, we are still testing their software, so make sure they don't get into the office. Oh, let me introduce you to the crew. Lumina is the blue and white one, but she hangs out in the vents for some reason most of the time (like I said, software is in beta). She won't come in through the doors, so if she tries to come in through the vents, use your sonar blast to scare her off, it sends a frequency to her program that sends her away from the office. The vent is above your head, so check in on her with the security system. Next up on the roster is Mark. His code name is Shadow because hes the dark black one. There's also Jaqob. His code name is Golden, because, duh, hes Golden. Last but not least, we got Melty. He's the man himself. Oh, if you're wondering why they look all torn up and trashy, its because we got them discount from the thrift store on 3rd street. We are really low on funds. Last thing before I go, no, you can't have overtime. We won't be able to pay you. Your shift is over at 6:00am sharp. We are trying to find another gaurd for the weekend, but if we can't you can come in saturday for a 6th shift. The job is pretty boring and not much happens, but you are safe and get paid, so bring some yarn and knit a sweater. The phone bill is going to be bigger than a polar bear after Thanksgiving, so I'm going to hang up. Please don't call me, it costs a lot.

Controls

Sensitivity

Before pressing play, mouse sensitivity can be changed in the settings menu. (Gear icon in the top right corner)


Look Around

To look around your office, move your mouse to the sides of the screen.


Doors

To turn on lights and close doors, left-click while your mouse is on the buttons.


Security System

To open your security system, click on the button at the bottom of your screen (grey) or press 'q'. To navigate between cameras, either click on the numbered buttons or press the corresponding number on your keyboard.


Sonar Blast

To pull up the sonar blast, either click the green button on the bottom of the screen or press 'e'. To blast, click the orange button.


Generator and Break Box

To open or close the breaker box, click the button that is labeled "open/close".

To use the generator, click and hold the now open breaker box.

The vertical rectangle next to the breaker box will fill up with green while you are charging.

If you stop, your progress will be reset. Note: This will only work if you are below 15% power.
